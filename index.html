Recipe

Chocolate Cake

Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 mins 25 mins 35 mins

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Beat the eggs.

2

 Melt the butter.

3

 Mix the all ingredients.

4

 Pour into mould.

5

 Place in oven and bake for 30 mins. and longer and longer until the cake is burnt
